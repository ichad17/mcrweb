Simple, responsive, performant websites with the fundamentals

I'm a firm believer in the power of a solid foundation. While the landscape of web technologies is constantly evolving, the core principles of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript remain as relevant and powerful as ever. Oftentimes, the old ways truly are the best. Simple, hand-coded websites don't need to be boring or dry; they can be dynamic, engaging, and highly effective. And for many projects, especially brochure sites or smaller web applications, you don't need the added complexity and overhead of computationally expensive frameworks to deliver an exceptional user experience.

That's why I love building with the fundamentals. It allows for a level of predictability and performance that's hard to match when you're relying on layers of abstraction. Every line of code is intentional, every element carefully considered. This hands-on approach translates directly to websites that load quickly, respond instantly, and provide a seamless experience for the user. It's about prioritizing efficiency and delivering a product that's both lightweight and powerful without sacrificing ambition or creativity.

Moreover, building with the fundamentals is fantastic for SEO. Search engines love clean, well-structured code, and hand-coding with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript allows for complete control over the markup and site architecture. This means I can optimize every detail to ensure your website ranks well in search results, driving organic traffic and maximizing visibility. There's no need to wrestle with a framework's opinionated structure; I can build precisely what search engines are looking for, giving your site a natural advantage.
